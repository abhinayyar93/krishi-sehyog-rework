Our Commitments
Convenience
Farmers can manage selling of their crops from simple clicks from their smartphones. Farmers can easily connect with retailers, wholesalers, restaurants, cafes, food processors, canteens, grocery stores and hostels. Buyers can place the order through our platform and leave the rest to us. Just a few clicks and our sourcing team will work diligently to fulfil your order. Just sit back and focus on your business.
Transparency
As we believe in complete transparency, the details of the buyers and sellers will always be available. And the order details will also be updated at every step. We have no hidden fees. Farmers and sellers set and manage the prices. We will charge a small fee with each transaction to facilitate the running of the platform.
Ease-of-use
Our platform will provide a simple and intuitive user experience to both the farmers and the consumers. This will simplify complex business processes with time-saving functionality and regularly generated reports.
Message from the founders
Coming from the families of farmers, we aim to build long, meaningful and fulfilling relationships between our farmers and our customers. Our values impact how we work. We are prepared to take the challenge of bringing the change. In the long run, we will make the crop trading procedure easy and efficient, benefitting everyone, leading to nurturing of our communities.
