A marketplace for farmers and consumers where commodity trading is made convenient, transparent and fast.
Selling crops to consumers made easy
Krishi Sehyog provides a convenient application where farmers can easily manage crop’s details and prices. We provide direct control over prices to the farmers so as to provide the best prices and cut out the in-between expenses. We guarantee to provide timely payments and easy transport directly to the consumers.
Buying fresh crops from local farmers made easy
Krishi Sehyog’s aim is to provide the best crops directly from local farmers from a choice of fresh fruits, vegetables and cereals. We will work directly with the consumers to understand their demands and to provide them with the best deals. You can sit back and focus on your business and let us handle sourcing the crops to you.
Transparent trading of crops
Krishi Sehyog believes in complete transparency and security. We provide the details of the buyer at every moment to the seller. We also undergo a rigorous verification process for our buyers and sellers, so that everyone can get the best prices and be assured that their crops are in safe hands.

How our platform works

For Farmers

  6. Stay updated by email or notification center

  5. Decide upon the buyers terms and regulations
